Sync your Garmin paddling activities to Kanu-EFB

EFB Connector automatically downloads your kayaking and canoeing activities from Garmin Connect and uploads them to your Kanu-EFB electronic logbook — every day, without any manual steps.

Get started — it's free

Automatic daily sync

Your activities are synced automatically around 04:00 UTC every day. No need to remember to upload manually.

No password required

Sign in with a magic link sent to your email — no password to create or remember.

Secure credential storage

Your Garmin and EFB credentials are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM.

How it works

  1. Sign in with your email address
  2. Connect your Garmin Connect account
  3. Connect your Kanu-EFB account
  4. Sit back — your activities sync automatically
