Sync your Garmin paddling activities to Kanu-EFB — automatically

EFB Connector downloads your paddling activities from Garmin Connect, uploads them to Kanu-EFB, and creates logbook entries with river data — every day, hands-free.

Get started — it's free

Automatic daily sync

Activities are synced from Garmin Connect to Kanu-EFB every day at ~04:00 UTC. No manual uploads needed.

Trip logbook entries

Automatically create Fahrten (logbook entries) in Kanu-EFB from your synced tracks, with start and end times pre-filled.

River data enrichment

Trip entries are enriched with river section name, difficulty grade, and current gauge readings from Rivermap.

Passwordless sign-in

No password to create or remember. Sign in with a magic link sent to your email address.

Encrypted credentials

Your Garmin and EFB credentials are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. You can remove them at any time.

Manual sync & history

Trigger a sync on demand, sync a custom date range, and review your full sync history with detailed status.

Supported activities

EFB Connector syncs these Garmin activity types:

Kayaking Canoeing Stand Up Paddleboarding Rowing Whitewater Rafting Paddling
